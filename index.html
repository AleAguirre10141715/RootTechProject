#5 Spider-Man: Far From Home

Spider-Man:Far From Home is the seond movie of Spider-Man starring Tom Holland. The reason I enjoy watching this movie is because as a teenager I can kind of relate to their problems. Peter Parker has to decide wether he is going to do what he wants to do or do the right thing and I feel that that is a common decision many teenagers have to face. Peter Parker also gets betrayed by the person that he trusted and felt connected to which is also something many teenagers go through. The best part of the movie was being able to see how Peter Parker was able to fix his mistakes and learn from them.

#4 Guardians Of The Galaxy

I realy enjoy how the characters are all sarcastic and how each of them have an individual mission they want to accomplish. What I most enjoyed from this movie was seeing how the characters slowly started to become a family. Each charater has their own flaws but they really complememt each other as a group. My favorite scene from this movie was the dance battle at the end of the movie. It was hilarious to see how a dance battle saved the universe.
